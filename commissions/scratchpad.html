Commission Styles & Prices


All my prices are calculated in the following manner: base price * (sum of finish tiers + specific price). So for example, a cel shaded thigh up would be $15 * (1 + 0.25(t1) + 0.25(t2) + 0.3(celshade) ) = $27. This price does not include add-ons and additional charges like lineart, design, complexity, or rush fees. Price is by character; very simple or tiny characters may be discounted. Unless otherwise stated, all commissions are at an appropriate quality to print!
