Commission Styles & Prices


All my prices are calculated in the following manner: base price * (sum of finish tiers + specific price). So for example, a cel shaded thigh up would be $15 * (1 + 0.25(t1) + 0.25(t2) + 0.3(celshade) ) = $27. This price does not include add-ons and additional charges like lineart, design, complexity, or rush fees. Price is by character; very simple or tiny characters may be discounted. Very large or detailed props may also warrant an additional charge.

Unless otherwise stated, all commissions are at an appropriate quality to print!

Looking for something else?

I'm willing to do almost anything in my portfolio and possibly some more! Let me know and we can discuss how prices will go.

Character Design

Character or costume design. You will get one character sheet with 2 full bodies with minimal shading, either front + back or outer + inner outfit layers, plus up to 3 detail shots or head shots. Additional detail shots can be added for $15 each.

Object design

Weapon or object design. You will get a sheet with as many full images or detail shots as I deem necessary for the design. Please note that I like to root my weapons in realism! You may ask for features not possible in real life... but I will do my hardest to make it make sense IRL.

Landscape Art

No characters, just background. Price will vary based on the detail and subject. I like painting natural things and I don't like painting rocks or cities.

Twitch/ Discord Emotes

Price per expression. Will include a free emote if buying 5+! These emotes are okay for commercial (twitch sub) use, but not for distribution or resale, including patreon rewards.
