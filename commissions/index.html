Terms of Service

Hey, like my stuff? Consider commissioning me!

While I currently don't have any prices or terms for web design or similar coding/design projects, I'm open to discuss if you're interested in what I do and would like me on board for one of your projects!

Process

  1. Read my Terms of Service.
  2. Send me an email, twitter, or instagram DM with this order form:
    What kind of art you want:
    What do you want drawn:
    References:
    Payment type: (Upfront | When starting | Split)
    Paypal email:
    Did you read and agree to my terms of service?
  3. After confirming details, I will send you a Paypal invoice and add you to my commission queue. You may pay it now, or pay it when I'm ready to begin work.
    IMPORTANT: My commission queue is a priority queue and first paid first served. Rush orders are highest priority, and below that, upfront USD > unpaid USD > non USD. That means, lower priority comms will get shifted back in favor of higher priority work. Only upfront USD orders will guarantee their spot in the queue.
  4. WIPs will be sent as I work– typically at pose sketch, final sketch, and flat colors stages. Edits can be asked for only at that current stage: I will not make sketch edits after colors have been done and I will not make color edits after I begin shading.
  5. You will be asked to confirm the final sketch before I start colors; I will not continue working without confirmation. Failure to confirm within 14 days will result in the commission being dropped. If you asked for split payment, you will get a second paypal invoice at this point– failure to pay within 14 days will result in the commission being dropped as well.
  6. Your final piece will be delivered by email to the paypal email you paid with. If the piece has additional requirements, such as printing or merch, I will continue to work with the production company and ship it to the address listed on your Paypal. If you need a different address, please request that through email only.

Commission Queue

Please note commissions tend to take longer than artwork I do for myself! I typically finish roughly 2-3 commissions per month. USD commissions and personal gifts typically take priority over virtual currency commissions.
