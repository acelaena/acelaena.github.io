Merch Styles & Prices


All prices for merch design are for the art and design only. I will not be responsible for the cost of manufacutre, shipping, or any VAT or other customs/import tax that may be incurred.

I have two different options for merch commissions: personal use and commercial use. For personal use, you will not get the original art files; I will work with one of my manus to produce the minimum possible pieces of merch and ship directly to you. Usually this minimum is somewhere around 3-10 pcs, but may be higher for specialty products. You may sell the extras, for no higher than 2x the unit cost of the item. For commercial use, I will not be responsible for sourcing a manu or working with them on production unless asked to beforehand. Please find a manu and acquire their file formatting specifications and I will format the files as they require. Commercial use prices are +150% for all acrylic and non-apparel fabric products, +500% for apparel, and +300% for everything else.
