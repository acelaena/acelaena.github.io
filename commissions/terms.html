Commission Terms of Service

  1. The artist reserves the right to decline the commission or cancel the commission and refund the comissioner at any time.
  2. The artist reserves the right to post commissions on social media and produce merchandise unless otherwise agreed upon.
  3. The artist will finish works at their own pace. If a deadline is to be met, a rush fee may be charged.
  4. Two options for payment: Full upfront, when the artist is ready to start the work, or 50% upfront, 25% after sketch confirmation, and 25% when the work is completed. For split payment, if any portion is below $10, Paypal fees will be charged. Payment is accepted in USD through Paypal only, except when otherwise mentioned. (See bottom of page for virtual currency commissions)

    Partial refunds will be given based on % completion of the artwork when requested. No refunds will be given after the artwork is complete.
        *If the artist deems themselves unable to complete the commission in a reasonable timeframe, full refunds will be offered.
  5. All references should be provided at time of order. Inadequate references may be subject to additional charges.
  6. Two edits will be allowed during the sketch phase. Edits after the sketch has been confirmed is up to the artist's discretion, and may be charged depending on how much needs to be changed. No edits will be done after the artwork is complete.
  7. Additional Charges: Commissions may be subject to an additional charge; see details below. The commissioner agrees to pay these charges along with the second half of the payment, or the commission will be dropped.
  8. Commissions are for private use only. The artist's signature must be visible at all times, and be visibly credited and linked to this site or tagged on-site if posted online. The commissioner may print the artwork for their own personal uses, and may not resell, distrubute, mass produce the artwork, or use the artwork for other commercial uses. For commercial inquiries please email at gildedserpentdesigns@gmail.com for details.
        *Personal use is defined as use online not generating revenue, or as print items not intended to be distributed. Mass production is defined as producing more than five copies of the art in print in any medium.

Pricing & Additional Fees

  1. All prices listed on the Commissions page are base prices only.
  2. Complexity Fees -- All complexity fees depend on coverage of complex elements
    • Complex outfit                    +$10-30
      > Anything involving steampunk, filigree/jewelry, armor, feathers, ruffles
    • Crystals, flowers, braids      +$10-20
    • Patterned fabric, lace           +$10-20
    • Wings/tail/etc                       +$10
    • Tattoos, body markings        +$10
    • Complex pose                       +$10-20
  3. Design Fees -- If no design is provided, or the references aren't 1:1 what to draw
    • $40 for full design from description or mood board
    • $20 for gijinka from other existing design
    • $5-10 if missing details/elements, such as fullbody from half body reference
  4. Rush Fees -- +10% additional price for every week of rush, down from 3 months. Minimum 1 week for sketches, 2 weeks for portraits and drawings, 3 weeks for paintings.
