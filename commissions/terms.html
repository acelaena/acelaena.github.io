Commission Terms of Service


General Terms

  1. The artist reserves the right to decline the commission or cancel the commission and refund the comissioner at any time.
  2. The artist reserves the right to post commissions on social media unless otherwise agreed upon.
  3. The artist will finish works at their own pace. If a deadline is to be met, a rush fee will be charged.
  4. All references should be provided at time of order. Inadequate references may be subject to additional charges.
  5. Two edits will be allowed during the sketch phase. Edits after the sketch has been confirmed is up to the artist's discretion, and may be charged depending on how much needs to be changed. No edits will be done after the artwork is complete.
  6. Significant changes in art style, or composition are not allowed after the sketch has been approved. If you would like to change the art style or request a style the artist doesn't normally paint in, this must be negotiated prior to payment, or refunds will not be offered for downgrades. An additional style change fee will be charged for upgrading styles.

Payment

  1. Two options for payment: Full upfront, when the artist is ready to start the work, or 50% upfront and 50% after sketch confirmation. For split payment, if any portion is below $10, a 10% surcharge will be applied. Payment is accepted in USD through Paypal only, except when otherwise mentioned. (See bottom of page for virtual currency commissions)

    Partial refunds will be given based on % completion of the artwork when requested. No refunds will be given after the artwork is complete.
        *If the artist deems themselves unable to complete the commission in a reasonable timeframe, full refunds will be offered.
  2. Additional Charges: Commissions may be subject to an additional charge; see details below. The client agrees to pay these charges after the sketch confirmation, or the commission will be dropped.

Pricing & Additional Fees

  1. All prices listed on the Commissions page are base prices only. If commercial rates are not listed, please email with an inquiry on what it will be used for.
  2. Complexity Fees -- All complexity fees depend on coverage of complex elements

    • Crystals, flowers, braids      +$10-20
    • Wings/tail/etc                       +$10
    • Tattoos, body markings        +$10

  3. Background -- All fees depend on coverage of background elements
    • Florals and foliage               +$10-50
    • Water                                   +$10-30
    • Cityscapes                           +$50-80
      > Includes anything that requires extensive geometric precision; not limited to just cityscapes
    • Extensive natural rock         +$5-15
    • Intricate housing/interior     +$5-35
  4. Design Fees
    • $40 for full design from description or mood board

  5. Rush Fees -- +10% additional price for every week of rush, down from 3 months. Minimum 1 week for sketches, 2 weeks for portraits and drawings, 3 weeks for paintings. This pricing scales off your subtotal including all additional fees, not the base pricing of the piece. Time for rush pricing starts after the initial invoice has been paid. The artist won't be responsible for missed deadlines due to the commission being put on hold due to delayed communication.
