Commission Terms of Service

General Rules

  1. Payment in USD through Paypal only. Virtual currency commissions are occasionally open on specific sites.
  2. Commissions are private use only. Please email me if you have business inquiries.
  3. USD commissions will always take precedent over virtual currency commissions.
  4. Will draw: Humanoids, fantasy animals, landscapes, gijinkas, crystals/jewels, most weapons, most objects.
  5. Will draw, but not a specialty: Anthro, NSFW (gore, nudity), animals (real life), guns (any kind), excessive armor/detailed filigree.
  6. Won't draw: Cityscapes, mech, NSFW(sexually explicit).
  7. I do commissions at my own pace; if you have a specific deadline please let me know and I will try to accomodate that. A rush fee may be charged.
  8. If there's any specific requests you may have (don't post, etc), please let me know prior to ordering.
  9. Please provide all reference at time of order! Inadequate references may be subject to an additional charge.
  10. When posting, please credit directly on-site if possible. If not please link to this site. Reposts are not permitted on Deviantart, Pinterest, or Zerochan/similar sites. (If reposts are seen, please report them to me!)

Terms of Service

  1. The artist reserves the right to decline the commission or cancel the commission and refund the comissioner at any time.
  2. The artist reserves the right to post commissions on social media and produce merchandise unless otherwise agreed upon.
  3. The artist will finish works at their own pace. If the work has not been started after three months, the commissioner may request a refund.
  4. The commissioner will pay 50% of the full payment upfront. The remaining 50% will be due after the first sketch is sent. Partial refunds will be given based on % completion of the artwork when requested. No refunds will be given after the artwork is complete.
        *If the artist deems themselves unable to complete the commission in a reasonable timeframe, refunds will be offered.
  5. Two edits will be allowed during the sketch phase. Edits after the sketch has been confirmed is up to the artist's discretion. No edits will be done after the artwork is complete.
  6. Details/Design/Edits Charge: Commissions may be subject to an additional charge if the design is highly detailed, for designing if adequate image references are not provided, or for additional major edits. The commissioner agrees to pay these charges along with the second half of the payment, or the commission will be dropped.
        *Extra charges will not exceed the base price of the commission.
  7. Commissions are for private use only. The artist's signature must be visible at all times, and be visibly credited and linked in text if posted online. The commissioner may print the artwork for their own personal uses, and may not resell, distrubute, mass produce the artwork, or use the artwork for other commercial uses. For commercial inquiries please email at gildedserpentdesigns@gmail.com for details.     *Personal use is defined as use online not generating revenue, or as print items not intended to be distributed. Mass production is defined as producing more than five copies of the art in print in any medium.
