Design Styles & Prices


All prices without an existing style guide +150%. All prices for commercial use (including twitch/youtube/other streaming, any online store, or any sort of print material) +300%. If you've previously commissioned me and are now looking to obtain these rights, the additional cost would be equal to my current prices, not whatever they were at the time of commission.

All prices include up to two revisions. Prices do not include cost of drawn assets, commercial license of fonts, or my copy of commercial font licenses. For emotes, chibis, and pngtubers, please see my art comms.

For series of graphics using the same assets, 60% discount on additional pieces. (ie. if only the text/color needs to be changed) If you've commissioned me in the past year and are looking for an update or additional pieces, a 20-60% discount may be applicable depending on how much existing assets I have to reuse.

NFT or other blockchain use is prohibited.

Looking for something else?

I'm willing to do almost anything in my portfolio and possibly some more! Let me know and we can discuss how prices will go.

Character Design

Character or costume design. You will get one character sheet with 2 full bodies with minimal shading, either front + back or outer + inner outfit layers, plus up to 3 detail shots or head shots. Additional detail shots can be added for $15 each.

Object design

Weapon or object design. You will get a sheet with as many full images or detail shots as I deem necessary for the design. Please note that I like to root my weapons in realism! You may ask for features not possible in real life... but I will do my hardest to make it make sense IRL.

Landscape Art

No characters, just background. Price will vary based on the detail and subject. I like painting natural things and I don't like painting rocks or cities.
